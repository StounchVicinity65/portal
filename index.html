⚛️ Physics — Motionপদার্থবিজ্ঞান — গতি

Equations of motion under constant acceleration:

v = u + at
s = ut + ½at²
v² = u² + 2as

u=initial velocity · v=final velocity · a=acceleration · t=time · s=displacement

📖 History: Galileo Galilei (1564-1642) revolutionized motion studies through experimentation with inclined planes. Newton's 3 laws (1687) unified terrestrial & celestial mechanics. Einstein later showed motion is relative to reference frames (1905).

Key Concepts:

• Projectile Motion: Objects under gravity follow parabolic paths. Horizontal & vertical motions are independent.

• Circular Motion: Centripetal force = mv²/r directs toward center. Angular velocity ω = 2π/T.

• Momentum: p = mv. Conservation: total momentum before = after collision.

• Energy: KE = ½mv². PE = mgh. Total mechanical energy conserved in closed systems.

🧪 Chemistry — Periodic Trendsরসায়ন — পর্যায়বৃত্ত প্রবণতা

Atomic Properties Across the Periodic Table:

Across period → radius ↓, ionization ↑, electronegativity ↑
Down group → radius ↑, ionization ↓, electronegativity ↓

Effective nuclear charge increases left→right, shielding increases top→bottom.

📖 History: Dmitri Mendeleev (1869) organized 63 known elements by atomic weight, leaving gaps for undiscovered elements. Modern periodic table (118 elements) reflects electron configuration. Moseley's X-ray work (1913) confirmed atomic number ordering.

Key Concepts:

• Oxidation States: Track electron transfer in reactions. Helps balance redox equations.

• Chemical Bonding: Ionic (electron transfer), Covalent (sharing), Metallic (electron sea).

• Stoichiometry: Mole ratios from balanced equations. Limiting reactant determines yield.

• Thermochemistry: ΔH = energy change. Exothermic (ΔH<0), Endothermic (ΔH>0).

🧬 Biology — Life & Evolutionজীববিজ্ঞান — জীবন ও বিবর্তন

Central Dogma of Molecular Biology:

DNA → RNA → Protein

DNA replication ensures genetic continuity. mRNA transcription transfers info. tRNA translation builds proteins.

📖 History: Darwin (1859) proposed natural selection as evolution's mechanism. Mendel (1860s) discovered inheritance laws through pea plants. Watson & Crick (1953) unveiled DNA's double helix. Modern synthesis combines genetics, population dynamics & evolutionary theory.

Key Concepts:

• Photosynthesis: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂. Light reactions (thylakoid) & Calvin cycle (stroma).

• Cell Cycle: Interphase (G1, S, G2) → Mitosis → Cytokinesis. Checkpoints prevent errors.

• Protein Synthesis: 64 codons encode 20 amino acids. Ribosomes read mRNA triplets.

• Evolution: Mutation, selection & drift drive allele frequency changes. Speciation via reproductive isolation.
